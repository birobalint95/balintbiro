As the technical manager, I was responsible for either coming up with new technical processes that will make the team's operation more effective, or finding the bottlenecks of our already existing processes and optimizing them. We followed the traditional V-model during this project. We went through the concept, design, manufacturing and assembling phases in order and then we maintained both an electic and an autonomour racecar and participated in international racing events.

Main responsibilities
  • Develop and improve technical processes and their platforms
    • Determination and implementation of the design method into the team's life
  • Making the trade-off between time, costs and extra technical performance
    • Setting up the time schedule, keeping the deadlines, having solution to delays
    • Ensure finishing on time on car level
  • Holding the Vehicle Level meeting
Main achievements
  • Newly introduced technical processes (with demonstration and documentation)
  • Racecar finished in time
  • Participation on international competitions
  • Designed, manufactured and assembled steering actuator (as my thesis)
